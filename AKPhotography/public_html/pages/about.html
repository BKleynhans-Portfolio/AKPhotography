This is me

Allison Kleynhans Photography

Really founded in 2004 when I was only 6 years old and found my parents "old school" camera. No toys could satisfy me in the way the camera did.

My photographs are based on my beliefs, all things are beautiful in their own way, you just have to find the thing that makes them unique. I rarely take posed photos, 'cause that's just what they are... posed and unreal.

Capturing real emotions and actions happen in real time and in the spur of the moment. I'm a spur of the moment, creative and enthusiastic, fun loving woman and so is my work. If I want to capture an event whether it be a wedding, birthday party or toddlers, I know that I would like to portray myself and my family/friends as I know them to be every day. These people are invited because they are happy, friendly, sometimes even silly and I would like to carry the real memories with me for the rest of my life...
